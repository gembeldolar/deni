The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Macaroni and Beef/Chicken:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 lb Lean ground beef or ground chicken
  2. Get 2 cup Uncooked elbow macaroni
  3. Prepare 1 medium Onion, chopped
  4. Take 1 can (4 oz.) Sliced mushrooms, drained
  5. Get 1 can (16 oz.) Whole kernal corn, drained (optional)
  6. Prepare 2 can Condensed tomato soup
  7. Prepare 1 can Diced tomatoes (14 1/2 oz.), drained reserving liquid.
  8. Make ready 1 Salt and pepper, to taste
Instructions to make Crockpot Macaroni and Beef/Chicken:
  1. In skillet, brown ground beef or ground chicken. Drain well and add to crockpot.
  2. Cook macaroni according to directions on package UNTIL BARELY TENDER! Drain well.
  3. Add macaroni and the remaining ingredients to crockpot, stirring just enough to blend. Cover and cook on low 4-6 hours or on high 2-3 hours, adding reserved juice as needed. Great with some bread and butter! Enjoy!
