The ingredients needed to make Crockpot chicken tacos:
  1. Get 1 pkg chicken breast. skinless and boneless
  2. Take 2 can diced tomatoes.. with lime and cilantro
  3. Take 2 garlic cloves
  4. Prepare 1 chili powder
  5. Make ready 1 ground cumin
  6. Get 1 salt pepper onion powder garlic powder

Instructions to make Crockpot chicken tacos:
  1. Put a can of diced tomatoes in bottom of crock pot.. add a lil of all seasonings. And one garlic clove..
  2. Place chicken in crockpot.. add second can on top.. add a lil bit of all seasonings.. and second garlic clove..
  3. Cook on high for 4 to 5 hours..
  4. Shred chicken and put back in juices.. warm up tortillas do not fry.. and make tacos.. add cheese lettuce avocado and lime.
