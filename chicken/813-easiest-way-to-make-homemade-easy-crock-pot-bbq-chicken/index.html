The ingredients needed to make Easy Crock Pot Bbq Chicken:
  1. Make ready 2 tbsp paprika
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp chili powder
  3. Get 1 tbsp kosher salt
  4. Take 1 tbsp ground cumin
  5. Get 1 tbsp coriander
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp granulated garlic
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp brown sugar
  8. Take 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  9. Get 1 tbsp onion powder
  10. Prepare 1 your favorite bbq sauce
  11. Get 2 lb Yukon potatoes
  12. Get 1 each whole chicken
Steps to make Easy Crock Pot Bbq Chicken:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients
  2. Cover chicken with spice mixture, put in frig for 2 hours, or over night is the best.
  3. Cut up potatoes, put in bottom of crock pot.1. Add chicken, cook on high for 4 hours. Add bbq sauce on chicken, cook another 1 hour.
  4. Enjoy
