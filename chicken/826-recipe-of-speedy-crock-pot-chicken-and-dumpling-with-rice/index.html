The ingredients needed to make Crock pot Chicken and dumpling with rice:
  1. Get dumpling
  2. Make ready 1/2 tsp cajun seasoning
  3. Prepare 1 cup bisquick
  4. Take 2/3 cup milk
  5. Take chicken
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 lb chicken breast, deboned.skinless
  7. Take 3 cup boiling water
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp lemon pepper seasonings
  9. Prepare 1/2 yellow onion
  10. Prepare 1 salt and pepper to taste
  11. Make ready 1 tsp minced roasted garlic
