The ingredients needed to make Baked Macaroni & Cheese (Surf & Turf):
  1. Make ready 1 lb. Pasta (I had Rigatoni)
  2. Get 3/4 lb. Smoked Salmon
  3. Make ready 1 Can Sweet Peas
  4. Make ready 1 Can Whole Peeled Tomatoes
  5. Prepare 1 Can Minced Clams
  6. Prepare 1/2 Can Spam
  7. Prepare 12 oz. Cheese (Roughly)
  8. Take Parmesan Cheese
  9. Get 1 Stick Butter
  10. Get 1/2 Cup Flour
  11. Prepare 4 Cups Milk
  12. Prepare Bread crumbs
  13. Get Additional Tbs. Butter
  14. Prepare Black Pepper
  15. Prepare Salt
  16. Make ready Oregano
  17. Prepare Garlic Powder
  18. Prepare Parsley
Instructions to make Baked Macaroni & Cheese (Surf & Turf):
  1. Preheat oven to 350 F. Prep ingredients and canned goods. Reserve clam juice. Start boiling water for pasta. Quarter tomatoes, drain liquid. Don‚Äôt feel too bad about losing the liquid. The tomatoes still have a lot of juice.
  2. Melt a stick of butter, add flour and continue to stir until you achieve a color as seen below. Add milk slowly while continuing to stir. Bring to a simmer and allow to thicken. Add a handful of cheese and combine.
  3. When roux has thickened, add it to a casserole dish of hopefully cooked al dente pasta.
  4. Add your prepped ingredients. Season. Mix throughly. Top with remaining cheese. Place in oven for 35-40 minutes.
  5. Add a tablespoon of butter into a pan with about 10 minutes to go on the timer. Melt butter and toast some bread crumbs. Mine were unseasoned… I seasoned them myself. In honesty, this step is totally optional.
