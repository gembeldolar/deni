The ingredients needed to make Mixed Greens, candied walnuts, feta &cranberry:
  1. Take 1 packages Mixed Greens
  2. Prepare 2 cup candied Walnuts
  3. Take 1 cup Feta cheese crumbles
  4. Prepare 1 cup Dried Cranberry
  5. Take 1 1/4 cup Champagne Vinaigrette
Instructions to make Mixed Greens, candied walnuts, feta &cranberry:
  1. Layer each ingredient over and over then drizzle with the dressing or toss and then add more toppings as garnish
  2. Serve immediately
