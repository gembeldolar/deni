The ingredients needed to make Mixed fried rice:
  1. Get 250 grams rice (non scented) boiled
  2. Take 1 tbsp chopped onion
  3. Prepare 1 cup spring onion
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup carrots cubed in small pieces
  5. Get 1/4 cup french bean diced
  6. Make ready 2 eggs
  7. Get 50 grams praws, deveined
  8. Get 1/2 cup chicken breasts, julliene
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp dark soya sauce
  10. Take 1/2 tbsp light soya sauce
  11. Get 1/2 tbsp ground black pepper
  12. Prepare 1 tsp ajinomoto
  13. Take salt
  14. Prepare 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps to make Mixed fried rice:
  1. Take a wok add a little oil. Make the egg scrambled. Set it aside.
  2. Fry the chicken add salt. And set it aside.
  3. Fry the prawns. Till its pink. Set it aside.
  4. Take a wok add the vegetables and fry.
  5. Take the boiled rice in a wok add 1,2,3 and 4 and stir. Until all is mixed well in low flame.
  6. Serve hot.
