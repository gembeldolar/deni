Instructions to make My Mixed Seafood & Veg Pie:
  1. Add everything to an oven proof dish with a lid except potatoes.
  2. Close the lid and add it to a preheated oven on 170 C or gas 6. For 45 minutes. In the midfle of the oven.
  3. Take out and stir pour some of the corn flour to the dish until its how thick you want it. It may be thickish already so just add a little at a time stirring. Then leave to cool
  4. Boil the potatoes until cooked then mash with butter, fresh chopped or dried parsley. Leave to go cool1. The fish sauce when cool leaves a thickisn layer on top, carefully add the mashed potatoe on top and spread all over gently
  5. Next using a fork run the fork up and down and prick the potatoe to lift up.then add the cheese.
  6. Pricked with a fork
  7. Cheese
  8. Add all back in the oven (when ready to eat) for 10 minutes on 190 C or gas 8. Until its golden on top and cheese has melted.
