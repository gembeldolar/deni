The ingredients needed to make Colorful Mixed Vegetables:
  1. Take 3 cup mixed vegetables (carrot,french beans, capsicum, baby corn, tomato, broccoli etc)
  2. Prepare salt
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp garlic (finely chopped)
  5. Make ready 1 onion, chopped
  6. Take 1/2 cup sweet corn
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  8. Take 1/2 tsp oyster sauce
  9. Prepare 1 tsp soy sauce

Instructions to make Colorful Mixed Vegetables:
  1. Heat oil in a non stick frying pan. Add mixed vegetables and salt. Stir fry for 5 min or until little tender.
  2. Add onion ,garlic, sweet corn and others. Cook until all vegetables are well cooked. Serve hot.
