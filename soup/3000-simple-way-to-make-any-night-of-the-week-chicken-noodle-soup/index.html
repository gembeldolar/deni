The ingredients needed to make Chicken noodle soup:
  1. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
  2. Get 1 cup chopped onions
  3. Prepare 1 cup chopped celery
  4. Prepare 1 cup chopped carrots
  5. Take 1 lb chicken breast (chopped in 1 inch pieces)
  6. Take 1 tbsp minced garlic
  7. Take 1 tsp thyme leaves
  8. Take 6 cup chicken broth
  9. Take 3 cup wide egg noodles

Instructions to make Chicken noodle soup:
  1. Once chicken is chopped in pieces season to your liking( example: salt, black pepper or poultry seasoning)
  2. In a large pot or dutch oven, heat oil over medium heat. Add carrots, celery, garlic and onions. Cook until onions are translucent
  3. Add chicken and thyme, cook for 2 1/2 minutes
  4. Add chicken broth and bring to a boil
  5. Add noodles and reduce heat. Let simmer for 12 minutes or until noodles and chicken are done.
