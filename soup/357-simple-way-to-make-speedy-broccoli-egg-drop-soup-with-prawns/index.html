The ingredients needed to make Broccoli egg drop soup with prawns:
  1. Prepare 1 kg prawns
  2. Get 2 pcs medium size fresh egg
  3. Make ready 1 kg broccoli bibs
  4. Take 1 pc onion
  5. Get 5 pcs cloves of garlic
  6. Take 1 tbsp or chopped ginger
  7. Take 2 pcs medium tomatoes
  8. Get 1 tbsp soy sauce
  9. Make ready pinch salt and black ground pepper
  10. Get little bit vegetable oil

Instructions to make Broccoli egg drop soup with prawns:
  1. Cut all the veggies into chunks 1. Sauté them in the pot until gets set,, drizzle with soy sauce, pour 4 cups of water and simmer it until everything get soft, for about 10 mins.,, when it's done,, set it aside to cool down a bit for blending it in a food processor.
  2. While on the other hand,, start to trim the prawn, and bit the eggs
  3. When the soup gets cooled, start to blend it in a processors..,, then simmer for another 5 mins in low heat
  4. Start to pour in the beaten eggs and prawns into the soup, season with salt and pepper to taste. serves as-well as with stir fried shrimp
