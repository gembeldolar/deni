The ingredients needed to make Lentil & potato pressure cooker vegetable soup:
  1. Make ready 8 cups broth
  2. Prepare 1 cup lentils (or barley or rice would probably work too.)
  3. Prepare 3 small potatoes, diced
  4. Get 2-3 cloves garlic
  5. Take 1-1/2 cup, approx, Celery, sliced,
  6. Get 2 onions, diced
  7. Take 1 tomato, diced
  8. Make ready ~1-1/2 cups green beans(or whatever other leftover veg you have)
  9. Get 1 dried habanero
  10. Prepare ~1 tbsp Butter
  11. Make ready ~1 tsp Olive oil
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp Paprika, at least
  13. Take to taste Oregano
  14. Get to taste Basil
  15. Make ready To taste pepper
  16. Make ready Dash thyme
  17. Take 2 bay leafs
  18. Take Dash Sherry if you've got it

Steps to make Lentil & potato pressure cooker vegetable soup:
  1. Set electric pressure cooker (Instant pot or other) to brown
  2. Melt butter and brown onions in it until they're good and caramelized on the outside.
  3. While onions are browning chop up any veggies that aren't prepared yet and the habanero pepper.
  4. Add all other ingredients to onions, including broth. Stir
  5. Set pressure cooker to high pressure for 18 minutes
  6. When time is up, fast release pressure and serve.
  7. Bon appetit
