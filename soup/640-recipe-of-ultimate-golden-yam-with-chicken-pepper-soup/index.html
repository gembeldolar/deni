Steps to make Golden Yam with chicken pepper soup:
  1. Peel your yam slice to desired shapes, wash and boil sieve out water in colander and close tight
  2. In a frying pan add enough oil and heat
  3. Break eggs and Add chopped small onion and salt. Whisk
  4. Add the boiled yam into the egg and turn gradually until well coated. Add in the hot oil. Fry until golden brown
  5. For the pepper soup. Wash and put in a pot. Add all ingredients and close lid until well cooked.
  6. And ta-da. Your food is ready
